Doja Cat

Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, known professionally as Doja Cat, is an American rapper and singer. She was born October 21, 1995 and was raised in Los Angeles. She began making music as a teenager, uploading her songs on Soundcloud. She soon caught the attention of Kemosabe and RCA Records, with whom she signed a recording contract to start creating music.
